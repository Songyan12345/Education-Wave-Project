Welcome to Education Wave

Efficient Online Tutoring System

At Education Wave aim to help those who are struggling with academic achievements and school work. We are an online tutoring company. We always strive to improve the students learning through high-quality one-on-one tuition. We operate as an online-only education provider. Our tutors and staff work from home to benefit others. We have a vast range of subjects that are covered, such as English, Mathematics, Physics, Biology, Computer Science, Accounting, and so on.


Why choose us?

The tutor and the student will form a healthy relationship which will increase the morale and confidence the student has. Giving them the courage to ask questions and correct their mistakes. One-on-one tutoring will beneficial to the individual as all the attention is directed towards them. The teachers adapt and change their teaching based on how the student is getting on. The tutors have a professional, friendly, and encouraging manner towards any student as they want to see the individual strive and succeed.


How does it work?

As this is an age of internet and technology, the whole tutoring system is based online. Choose your tutor, contact them via email to discuss the payment and price. They will give you a choice of communication, whether Skype, Zoom or another preferred call application. Each lesson, the tutor will send a pdf, which contains the details of the worksheet that needs to be completed either during that lesson or before the next lesson. They will go through the sheet and provide help when needed. The student is expected to have stationary ready before the day of the tutoring. Such as a pen, paper, and laptop/ computer.

About Us

Education Wave was established in 2020. It is a New Zealand operation with tutors from around the world. We are an innovative serivce that helps students that struggling with school work.
At Education Wave, our tutors are graduates from all types of Bachelor Degrees such as Bachelor of Commerce, Bachelor of Science, and more. We want to provide tutoring from the comfort of your home, where both parties can communicate online which makes it very convenient.

Our Goal

We want to train ambitious and aspiring students. We want to give students the best shot for their future. Giving them an opportunity to improve while they still young and have a chance. Setting the young kids on a path which will benefit everyone in the future. Our aim is also to create a healthy, friendly and fun environment where students will be happy to learn.
