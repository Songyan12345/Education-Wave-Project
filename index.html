Welcome to Education Wave

Efficient Online Tutoring System for High School Students

At Education Wave, we aim to help high school students who are struggling with academic achievements, interested in a new field, or would like to extend themselves. We are an online tutoring company who always strives to improve student learning through high-quality one-on-one tuition. We operate as an online-only education provider, with our tutors and staff working from home to benefit others. We have a vast range of subjects that we cover, such as English, Mathematics, and Computer Science.


Why choose us?

The tutor and the student will form a healthy relationship which will increase the morale and confidence of the student, giving them the courage to ask questions and correct their mistakes. One-on-one tutoring will beneficial to the individual as all the attention is directed towards them. The teachers adapt and alter their teaching methods based on the individual needs of each student. The tutors have a professional, friendly, and encouraging manner towards any student as they want to see the individual strive and succeed.


How does it work?

As this is an age of the internet and technology, the whole tutoring system is based online. Choose your tutor, and contact them via email to discuss the payment and price. They will give you a choice of communication, whether it'd be Skype, Zoom or another preferred call application. During each lesson, the tutor will send a PDF containing the details of the worksheet that needs to be completed either during that lesson or before the next lesson. They will go through the sheet and provide help if needed. The student is expected to have their own stationery ready before tuition, such as a pen, paper, and laptop/ computer.

About Us

Education Wave was established in 2020. A New Zealand operation with tutors from around the world, we are an innovative serivce that helps students with their studies in high school. At Education Wave, our tutors consist of tertiary students and graduates from various degrees such as the Bachelor of Commerce, Bachelor of Science, and more. We want to provide tutoring from the comfort of your home, where both parties can communicate online. This allows for a very convenient experience.

Our Goal

We want to teach ambitious and aspiring students. We want to give students the best shot for their future, giving them an opportunity to improve while they still young and setting them off on a path to benefit society in the future. Our aim is to create a healthy, friendly and fun environment where students will be happy and eager to learn.

Our Values

    In order to have a successful learning experience, we strongly stand by our values:

  • Respect and communicate
  • Friendly and safe environment
  • A passion in what you are studying
  • Ask when in doubt
  • Celebrate achievements and successes
